Music has been a long-term creative practice for me, alongside my work in AI and software engineering.

I am actively exploring **digital music production**, working with a digital audio workstation to create original beats and musical sketches. In addition to digital production, I have experience **playing musical instruments**, including guitar, which informs how I think about musical structure, timing, and expression. This hands-on engagement with music shapes how I approach sound not only as a creative medium, but also as a signal and a system.
